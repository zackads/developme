Here are some facts I bet you didn't know about cow pie:

  • Bison is actually the main meat ingredient.
  • The shortcrust pastry must be made with lard, not butter.
  • C. O. W. stands for Carne Obrigado Westphalia (Grateful Meat of North-western Germany).

So: "cow" pie actually has very little to do with cows!
